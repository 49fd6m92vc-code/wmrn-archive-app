WMRN Radio History Archive

Preserving the voices, people, and legacy of WMRN Radio.

Welcome to the WMRN Radio History Archive. This project documents and preserves recordings, photographs, documents, and personal memories connected to WMRN Radio and its role in Marion, Ohio and the surrounding region.

For generations, WMRN has helped shape the sound, stories, and shared experience of this community. This archive exists to ensure that history is not lost.

Explore the Archive

  • Browse WMRN history by decade
  • View historic photos from studios, events, and daily life
  • Listen to preserved broadcasts and recordings
  • Contribute materials, memories, or information

This is a living archive. New materials are added as recordings, photographs, and stories are discovered, preserved, and documented.
