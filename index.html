About the Archive

Welcome to the WMRN Radio History Archive. This project documents and preserves recordings, photographs, documents, and personal memories connected to WMRN Radio and its role in Marion, Ohio and the surrounding region.

For generations, WMRN has helped shape the sound, stories, and shared experience of this community. This archive exists to ensure that history is not lost.
